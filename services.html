🗺 Service 01

Transition Planning

Every successful transition begins with a solid plan — and every plan begins with deep listening. Before we talk about timelines or logistics, we sit down together to understand what you truly want, what your family needs, and what success looks like for everyone involved.

Our transition planning process creates a personalized roadmap that honors your goals, accounts for real-world constraints, and builds in the time needed for thoughtful decisions. No rushed choices. No surprises.

Whether you're planning six months out or two years, we'll help you see the full picture — and take it one clear step at a time.

What's Included
  • In-depth discovery consultation (in-person or virtual)
  • Goals & values alignment session
  • Customized transition timeline and milestone map
  • Needs assessment and living options analysis
  • Family communication strategy
  • Ongoing plan management and check-ins

Best For

  • Adults 55+ exploring next-chapter options
  • Families beginning the conversation early
  • Anyone who wants a clear picture before committing
  • Those facing a health or life event requiring change

Ready to create your transition plan? Let's start with a free conversation.

🏡 Service 02

Downsizing & Decluttering

Downsizing is rarely just about belongings — it is about memories, identity, and a lifetime of experiences. Every drawer and closet holds a story. Approaching this process with the care and patience it deserves is not a luxury; it is a necessity.

We guide individuals and families through the process of simplifying a home with compassion and structure — helping make clear, confident decisions about what to keep, what to gift, what to donate, and what to let go of.

We work at your pace, honoring what matters while creating the space for a fresh, lighter chapter ahead.

What's Included
  • Room-by-room assessment and sorting plan
  • Compassionate, non-judgmental guidance throughout
  • Family coordination for heirloom decisions
  • Donation coordination with vetted local organizations
  • Estate sale or consignment coordination (if needed)
  • Junk removal and final clean-out coordination
  • Documentation and photo record of items shared

Best For

  • Preparing a home for sale or rental
  • Moving from a large home to a smaller space
  • After the loss of a spouse or parent
  • Clearing a family home with emotional history

This is one of the most emotionally charged parts of any transition. We're here to help you through it.

📦 Service 03

Relocation Coordination

A successful move involves dozens of moving parts — literally and figuratively. From researching the right community to overseeing the final walkthrough of a new home, every detail matters. And when you're managing it while supporting a parent or navigating your own transition, it can feel impossible.

We coordinate every element of the relocation process so that nothing falls through the cracks — and so that moving day is an occasion to celebrate, not endure.

We work with both local and long-distance moves, and maintain strong relationships with senior-specialized moving companies and destination communities.

What's Included
  • Community research and comparison support
  • Move logistics planning and timeline management
  • Senior-specialized mover coordination
  • New home setup and furniture placement planning
  • Utility and service transfer coordination
  • Settling-in support and community orientation
  • Long-distance coordination and family communication

Best For

  • Moving to independent or assisted living
  • Relocating closer to family
  • Long-distance moves requiring extra oversight
  • Families who need a single point of contact

Let us manage the complexity so you can focus on what matters — the person you love.

👨‍👩‍👧 Service 04

Family Coordination

Major life transitions surface long-held family dynamics — sometimes bringing loved ones closer together, and sometimes creating conflict. When multiple family members are involved in a parent's transition, aligning on what's best requires patience, structure, and a neutral facilitator.

We help families have the right conversations at the right time — ensuring that everyone feels heard, that the parent's wishes remain central, and that decisions are made with love and clarity rather than urgency or conflict.

Our role is to bring calm to the conversations that feel impossible to start, and to create a shared plan that everyone can support.

What's Included
  • Family discovery calls and listening sessions
  • Structured family meeting facilitation
  • Communication frameworks for difficult topics
  • Documentation of shared decisions and agreements
  • Ongoing family communication management
  • Support for long-distance family members
  • Conflict de-escalation and alignment support

Best For

  • Families with multiple siblings involved
  • Situations where family members disagree
  • Parents who feel caught in the middle
  • Long-distance families needing a local presence

The best transitions bring families together. Let us help yours get there.
